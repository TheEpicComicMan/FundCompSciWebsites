The Saturn V!

NASA Article on the Saturn V

This is a Saturn V in KSP:

			
The Saturn V is the rocket that took Americans to the moon.
The five F-1 engines in the first stage (S-1C) are the most powerful engine ever made.

			
The Saturn V had three stages, the S-1C, S-2, and S-4B.
The first two and part of the third were for getting the craft into a parking orbit.
The third stage was light twice, the second time was to get the craft on a free-return trajectory around the moon.
